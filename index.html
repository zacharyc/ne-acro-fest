We are excited to welcome you to the first New England AcroFest (NEAF). Founded by Sarah Feinberg & Liticia Avery, NEAF was born out of a desire to create a fun, creative, & accessible container in our own backyard.

Upon entering this container, we agree to:

  1. Focus on the safety of those around us & ourselves
  2. Be open to new connections
  3. Treat others how you want to be treated
  4. Have fun!

COVID POLICY:

  1. MANDATORY pre-travel rapid COVID test. We are trusting our community and will not have the capacity to check for proof of rapid tests. However, anyone exhibiting symptoms may be requested to take a test and/or asked to leave the festival upon refusal. We reserve the right to use our judgment in such cases. We have a medical professional on staff to assist in such assessments.
  2. It is HIGHLY ENCOURAGED that you wear a mask while traveling and anytime you are with large crowds of people outside of the festival facility. Rapid home Covid tests are readily available and you should start making plans to have your test ready before the festival.
  3. We RECOMMEND being up to date on current vaccinations
  4. OPTIONAL masking in the facility
  5. Policy is subject to change based on CDC recommendations
